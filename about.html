Stéphane has a strange and varied history of jobs, world-views, and injuries. Growing up with a continental philosopher as a father, he pronounced early on that philosophy was not going to be his job. This, of course, set him up for a later doom of studying and teaching yoga and Buddhism. On the way there Stéphane worked in and crashed out of the first internet boom as a database manager, spent seven years working post-production on the Bachelor, met the woman who became his wife, became an itinerant student and teacher until settling as a yoga teacher in San Francsico in 2013. He likes, in no particular order and far from comprensively, Arya Nagarjuna, Thomas Pynchon, Macross, video games and not having the time for them, and the Bay Area. He loves wife and son and his extended family. Oh, Stéphane also seems to like programming. Let's hope that lasts!
